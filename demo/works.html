Overview

CommitPool is a self-commitment dApp designed to help you succeed. Just choose a goal and stake some cryptocurrency on your success! If you meet your goal, you get your money back—simple as that.

Using CommitPool

Step 1:

Make sure you have an Ethereum wallet. We recommend Torus for beginners, or Metamask for more advanced users.

Step 2:

Connect your Strava account. This lets us track the distance that you walk, run, or bike, so we can give you credit for your activity!

Step 3:

Make a commitment and set a time limit. You'll be asked to pick an activity type (e.g., running or biking), a distance (e.g., 15 miles), a stake (e.g., 30 DAI), and a time limit (e.g., 10 days).

Step 4:

Confirm your commitment. Your wallet will give you a pop-up confirming that you're signing this transaction—click yes/accept to send your commitment to the blockchain!

Step 5:

Fulfill your commitment. Make sure to track your activity using Strava, or else you won't get credit. Make sure to complete your goal within the set time limit!

Step 6:

Congratulations! If you met your goal, your money is unlocked and you can re-claim it. If not, it goes into the CommitPool DAO. Either way, be sure to create another commitment and try again!
